What is a Carbon Footprint?
And why should every shopper care about it

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases — primarily carbon dioxide (CO₂) and methane — released into the atmosphere as a direct result of a product's entire lifecycle. This includes mining raw materials, manufacturing, packaging, transportation, usage, and final disposal.

These gases act like a blanket around the Earth, trapping heat from the sun. As concentrations rise, global temperatures climb — causing melting glaciers, rising sea levels, droughts, floods, and loss of biodiversity. Scientists agree we must urgently reduce emissions to avoid catastrophic climate change.

Everyday shopping decisions add up. A single plastic bottle generates ~6 kg CO₂ over its life — equivalent to leaving a light bulb on for 60 hours. Multiply that by billions of purchases daily, and the impact is enormous. EcoScore helps you make the switch before you click "Buy Now."

🏭
Manufacturing Emissions
Producing plastic releases fossil fuels burned during extraction, refining, and molding — none of which is recoverable.
🚢
Transport & Shipping
Global supply chains move products thousands of km by ship, truck, and air — each kilometre adding CO₂ to your purchase.
🗑️
End-of-Life Waste
Non-biodegradable products sit in landfills for 400–1000 years, slowly leaching chemicals and methane into soil and air.
🌡️
Climate Impact
Every 1°C rise in temperature intensifies hurricanes, wildfires, and crop failures — threatening food and water security worldwide.
The good news? Choosing products made from bamboo, glass, steel, or recycled materials can reduce your carbon footprint by up to 10× per purchase. Small choices, compounded across millions of shoppers, create massive change.

🌿 Install EcoScore Extension

To install the **EcoScore Chrome Extension** in your browser, follow these simple developer instructions since it is in local development:
1
Open Extensions Settings: Open a new tab in Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions (or click Menu > Extensions > Manage Extensions).
2
Enable Developer Mode: In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, toggle the **"Developer mode"** switch to **ON**.
3
Load Unpacked Extension: In the top-left corner, click the **"Load unpacked"** button.
4
Select Project Folder: Browse and select the primary **"EcoScore Browser Extension"** folder from your local workspace.
🎉 Success! The green leaf icon will appear in your browser toolbar, and will instantly grade any product you search on Amazon or Flipkart.
